How to Delete

Steps to delete your account in the app

  1. Open the Global Football Challenge app and sign in to your account.
  2. Tap your Profile icon in the top corner of the home screen.
  3. Scroll down and tap Settings.
  4. Tap Delete Account at the bottom of the Settings screen.
  5. Read the confirmation message, then tap Confirm Delete to permanently remove your account.
⚠️ This action is permanent. Once your account is deleted, it cannot be recovered. All your ranking, match history, and progress will be lost.
